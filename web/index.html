How Polymarket pays vs. what PolyParlay shows. Polymarket pays each leg independently — there's no native "all-or-nothing" parlay contract. The combined multiplier is reference-only and only realises if you execute as a true parlay through a third-party contract. PolyParlay analyses — it doesn't execute.
